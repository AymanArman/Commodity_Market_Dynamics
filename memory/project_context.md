FIN 452 capstone project — building a Golem app as an interactive primer on commodity market dynamics for risk and portfolio managers unfamiliar with the space.

Graded on accuracy, quality, readability, usability, and creativity on an absolute basis. Going beyond the minimum analytical requirements is expected and rewarded.

**Why:** Assignment requires covering forward curves, volatility, cross-market relationships, seasonality, and hedging analytics. The app must tell a story — to the manager themselves, their team, and stakeholders.

**How to apply:** Scope decisions should favor depth and creativity over just meeting minimums. COVID and structural regime breaks should be flagged explicitly when stress-testing analysis — noted for planning phase. Hedging Analytics page has not been discussed yet as of 2026-03-30. Market Dynamics page commodity group content still being fleshed out.

**Current phase:** Still in brainstorming as of 2026-03-30. `brainstorm.md` is the active file. `planning.md` does not exist yet.
